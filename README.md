# Alvaro Altamirano Montoya

**Development economist and data scientist** working at the intersection of applied microeconomics, energy and fiscal policy, and large-scale data analysis across Latin America and the Caribbean.

Economics and Data Consultant at the **Inter-American Development Bank (IDB)**. Previously with the **World Bank** and the **Global Green Growth Institute (GGGI)**. MS in Data Science for Public Policy, **Georgetown University**.

---

### Focus areas

- **Energy economics and subsidy policy**  -  household energy consumption, fossil fuel revenue recycling, input-output modeling of price reforms
- **Labor markets and pensions**  -  social security simulation, actuarial modeling, redistributive analysis, workforce development across 20+ LAC countries
- **Poverty and distributional analysis**  -  household survey microdata, multidimensional poverty measurement
- **Applied ML and NLP**  -  text classification, skills taxonomies, web scraping for economic measurement
